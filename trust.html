In one paragraph.

The KYE Protocol™ contract is bank-grade and frozen. The reference implementation runs 396 automated tests green on every commit, ships 17 hardening middleware modules opt-in via env vars, ships an HSM/KMS-swappable key-custody adapter, ships a Postgres-backed append-only audit chain, ships Helm chart + Dockerfile, and ships 12 operator runbooks. The 266 control mappings across 13 frameworks and the OSCAL exporter are the substrate auditors and procurement teams use. What we will not claim until the artefacts exist: SOC 2 / ISO 27001 / FedRAMP attestation; live measured uptime; named Tier-1 customer references. Those are next steps on the roadmap, not present-tense claims.
