Boilerplate

Short: KYE Protocol™ is the open Entity Authority Protocol for AI governance — an Apache 2.0 contract that proves who or what is acting, on behalf of whom, using which capability, under what authority, in what state, and with what audit trail. Establishes Authority Finality™ for AI-agent actions.

Full: KYE Protocol™ — Know Your Entity™ — is the open standard that turns every AI-agent and automation action into signed, replayable evidence a regulator, court, auditor, or dispute panel can verify with public keys alone. v1.0 ships 36 v1.0 normative profiles + 5 v1.1 preview, 186 OpenAPI operations across 87+ runtime endpoints, 55 JSON Schemas, three SDKs (TypeScript / Python / Go), 38 conformance fixtures, and 280 compliance control mappings across SOC 2, ISO 27001:2022, PCI DSS 4.0, PSD2/PSD3, DORA, NIS2, EU AI Act, NIST 800-207, and HIPAA.
