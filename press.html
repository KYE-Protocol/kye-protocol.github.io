Boilerplate

Short: KYE Protocol™ is the Entity Authority Protocol for AI governance — a contract that proves who or what is acting, on behalf of whom, using which capability, under what authority, in what state, and with what audit trail. Establishes Authority Finality™ for AI-agent actions.

Full: KYE Protocol™ — Know Your Entity™ — turns every AI-agent action into signed, replayable evidence a regulator, court, or auditor can verify with public keys alone.

v1.0 contents: 57 normative profiles, 193 OpenAPI operations, 119 JSON Schemas, three SDKs (TypeScript / Python / Go), 41 conformance fixtures, 266 control mappings across 13 horizontal frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001:2022, PCI DSS 4.0, PSD2/PSD3, DORA, NIS2, EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, NIST 800-207, NIST CSF, GDPR, FedRAMP) plus sector overlays. The patent-safe public surface (vocabulary, schemas, examples) is mirrored to the public KYE-Protocol/* repos under Apache 2.0; the master repository at KYE-Protocol/app is proprietary.
