Integrations · what KYE composes with

KYE doesn’t replace your stack — it binds authority across it.

KYE Protocol™ is designed to compose with the identity, policy, agent runtime, payment rail, KYC / KYB / KYA, SIEM, GRC, and audit infrastructure you already run. Each integration carries a conformance status: shipping means a tested adapter or fixture exists today; reference pattern means a documented integration shape exists but no managed adapter; roadmap means scheduled for a v1.x preview.

KYC / KYB / KYA

Verification providers feed the entity record; KYE binds authority.

Identity-verification vendors are upstream of the KYE entity record. KYE does not duplicate KYC; it consumes the verification result + the verifier’s evidence URI and binds the result into the entity’s authority surface.
