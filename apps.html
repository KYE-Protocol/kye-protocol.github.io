Legal · 7 apps

Authority Finality™ for legally relevant actions.

KYE Legal Profile™ apps prove who or what acted, on behalf of whom, under what authority, inside what scope, in what state, and with what evidence when a legally relevant action occurs — contracts, e-signatures, agent-negotiated transactions, corporate approvals, legal holds, matter access, settlement authority, regulatory submissions, dispute evidence.

No legal finality without authority finality.

Non-goal: KYE™ does not replace lawyers, courts, e-signature platforms, or legal agreements. It provides the technical authority and evidence layer that legal validity, enforceability, dispute resolution, and audit defensibility depend on.

App manifest

Every app ships a kye.app_manifest.v1.

App manifests declare what the app does, which profiles it supports, deployment modes (hosted · BYOC · on-prem), the OSS / commercial component split, and the commercial model (annual licence + usage, transaction fees, bps).
