Step 4 · Schemas

JSON Schema. Bytes on the wire.

Steps 1–3 give names, hierarchy and meaning. Step 4 is the runtime contract: every KYE™ object on the wire validates against a JSON Schema 2020-12 file with a stable $id URL. ajv walks every example through the matching schema on every CI run.

1 · What lives here

103 published schemas. Validated in CI.

KYE™ schemas are versioned, namespaced under https://kye-protocol.github.io/schemas/, and grouped by profile family. Each schema has at least one example mirrored to public/examples/. The validator (node scripts/validate-schemas.js) walks every example through the matching schema on every CI run.

  • Core: entity, delegation, scope, policy-decision, runtime-event, trust-domain, identifier, relationship, access-right, credential, attestation, audit-event, proof-bundle, signal, transfer, compliance-card …
  • KYE Continuity Profile™: continuity-profile, continuity-context, intent-trace, continuity-decision, agency-drift-event, continuity-evidence-pack.
  • KYE Discoverability Profile™: discoverability-profile, directory-entry, discovery-query, discovery-result, discovery-policy, authority-path-discovery, discovery-audit-event.
  • KYE Ontology Profile™: ontology-profile, ontology-term, ontology-relationship, ontology-mapping, semantic-assertion, jsonld-context.
  • KYE Operating Model Profile™: operating-model-profile, use-case-intake, readiness-assessment, entity-authority-record, authority-gate, commit-boundary, review-path, training-record, adoption-evidence-pack, governed-catalog-entry.
  • KYE Assurance Card Profile™: assurance-card-profile, assurance-card, human-involvement-plan, provenance-evidence, assurance-review-cycle, decommissioning-plan.
