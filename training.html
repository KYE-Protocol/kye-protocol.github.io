Live workshops

Three formats, real artefacts.

Workshops produce signed artefacts you can keep — not slides. Each is delivered remotely or on-site by the KYE Protocol™ programme team.

  • W1Authority Mapping Workshop — 60–90 min for buyers/CIOs. We work through one of your real agent flows live and produce an authority map, a delegation map, a capability map, the risk areas, and a written pilot recommendation. The artefact is the deliverable.
  • W2Pilot Kickoff — half-day for enterprise engineers + architects. We scope a 2–4 week Authority Finality™ Pilot, draft the entity model, agree the conformance subset, and stand up the reference Gateway against your first agent flow.
  • W3Auditor Replay Lab — half-day for internal audit / 3PAOs. Hands-on: take a sample evidence pack, verify the signatures with public keys alone, project to OSCAL, walk the control mapping. End: the auditor can run the replay independently.

Train-the-trainer

For partner enablement.

L3 KYE Conformant™ and L4 KYE Certified™ partners can opt in to train-the-trainer enablement: deliver KYE Protocol™ workshops to your customers under a co-branding licence, with programme-supplied materials and signed completion artefacts. Two paths:

  • Workshop track — deliver Authority Mapping Workshops or Pilot Kickoffs. Programme provides the deck, the worked-flow library, and signed completion artefacts for attendees.
  • Implementation track — for SIs and consultancies running multi-week implementations. Programme provides the implementation playbook, the gap-analysis template, and a registry-listed implementer badge.

Certification preparation

For audit firms going to L4.

Audit firms preparing to deliver L4 KYE Certified™ audits complete a programme orientation: the 38-fixture conformance pack, the evidence-pack format, the OSCAL projection, the report template, and the audit-firm agreement. Two-day intensive, remote or on-site, signed completion artefact.
