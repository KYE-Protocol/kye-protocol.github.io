Authority Finality™

Replayable proof of who or what acted.

Every governed action answers six questions, signed: who or what is acting, on whose behalf, using which capability, under what authority, in what state, with what audit trail. The Decision Map™ visualises the answer per decision; the evidence pack carries the signed chain offline.
